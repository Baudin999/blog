As a Technical Director I am helping the development of an MMO RPG called Corepunk. Creating an MMO has focused all of my skills. Finding and hiring the right people, refactoring a legacy code-base and helping the stakeholders make decisions like "which e-commerce platform should we use?"

Exploring new technologies and ways to solve solutions. Creating a MMO RPG is hard.
